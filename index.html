Products

Silk Foundation

Description:

Silk Foundation is designed to deliver a smooth, even complexion while maintaining a natural skin-like finish. Its lightweight texture blends effortlessly into the skin.

Key Features:

  • Medium to full coverage
  • Natural radiant finish
  • Lightweight formula
  • Long-lasting wear

How to Use:

Apply a small amount to the face and blend evenly using a brush, sponge, or fingertips.

Velvet Lipstick

Description:

A richly pigmented lipstick that delivers intense color with a smooth, velvety texture and comfortable wear throughout the day.

Key Features:

  • High color payoff
  • Soft matte finish
  • Non-drying formula
  • Long-lasting

How to Use:

Apply directly to the lips or use a lip liner for a more defined look.

Volume Mascara

Description:

This mascara is designed to enhance lash volume and definition while maintaining a clean, clump-free finish.

Key Features:

  • Adds volume and length
  • Defines each lash
  • Clump-free application
  • Long-wear formula

How to Use:

Apply from the roots to the tips of the lashes using a zig-zag motion.

Glow Primer

Description:

Glow Primer prepares the skin for makeup while enhancing natural radiance and smoothing the complexion.

Key Features:

  • Hydrates the skin
  • Creates a glowing base
  • Smooths skin texture
  • Extends makeup wear

How to Use:

Apply to clean skin before foundation for a flawless base.

Blush Palette

Description:

A versatile blush palette featuring carefully selected shades to complement a wide range of skin tones.

Key Features:

  • Multiple shades in one palette
  • Fine, blendable texture
  • Natural-looking finish
  • Buildable color
